About Me

I was born in 2005 and developed a strong interest in computers in 2020, which marked the beginning of my journey into the world of technology. What started as curiosity quickly grew into a passion for understanding how computer systems work, how they can be secured, and how vulnerabilities can be identified and mitigated.

In 2024, I officially began my professional IT journey, focusing on cybersecurity—particularly ethical hacking and web security analysis. Since then, I have built hands-on experience in identifying and analyzing security weaknesses in web applications through multiple vulnerability assessments. I have also developed the ability to document findings clearly and professionally by writing detailed security reports that outline risks, impacts, and recommended solutions.

I am highly motivated to continue growing in the cybersecurity field, with a strong interest in penetration testing, digital forensics, and advanced threat analysis. My goal is to become a skilled cybersecurity professional who not only identifies vulnerabilities but also helps organizations build stronger, more resilient systems.
